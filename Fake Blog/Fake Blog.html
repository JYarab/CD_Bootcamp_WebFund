A Post About Nothing

and the joy it brings

Go for a walk in the wood. Somewhere, heading to no where just walk. Shut off your mind and just listen. Do nothing, just walk. No goal, no plan, no schedule. We have enough schedules, deadlines, meetings and obligations nagging at us every day. Escape them, forget them even if for just a few moments. Be free of the noise and remember what it's like to hear yourself.
